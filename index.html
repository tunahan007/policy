Decision Maker Policy

Privacy Policy for the Decision-Making App:

Our decision-making app is designed for entertainment purposes and is committed to protecting the privacy and personal data of its users. We do not collect any personally identifiable information, as the app neither displays advertisements nor requires user login.

Data Collection:

Our decision-making app does not gather any personal data from users. We do not record user activities or personal information. No data about the decisions made by users is stored or reused.

Use of Third-Party Services:

The app does not access external services for data collection or processing. No analytics services, advertising networks, or other third-party tools are utilized.

Age Restrictions:

As the app does not display content that is subject to age restrictions, there are no specific age requirements for using the app. The decision-making app is suitable for all age groups.

Changes to the Privacy Policy:

If our privacy policy changes, we will inform users accordingly and take all necessary steps to ensure the continued protection of user privacy. We prioritize our users' privacy and aim to provide a transparent and secure environment for app usage.
